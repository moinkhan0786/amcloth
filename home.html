Career Opportunities at A M Cloth House

A M Cloth House offers exciting career opportunities in the field of garment manufacturing. We are currently hiring for the following positions:

  • Embroidery Worker
  • Stone Paster
  • Stitcher
  • Quality Control (QC)

As an employer, we value skill, dedication, and a passion for quality workmanship. Joining our team means being part of a dynamic and collaborative environment where your contributions are valued and rewarded.

If you have experience in any of the mentioned positions or are eager to learn and grow in the garment manufacturing industry, we would love to hear from you. Please submit your resume and a brief introduction to careers@amclothhouse.com.

We offer competitive compensation packages, training opportunities, and a supportive work culture that promotes professional development. Join us on our journey to create high-quality Islamic wear for our valued customers.
